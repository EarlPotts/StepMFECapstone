History

In 1921, the Tulsa, Oklahoma Greenwood district was one of the most prosperpus black communities in the United States, which led it to be referred to as "Black Wall Street". However in May of that year it was reported that a black man assaulted a white woman and instead of waiting on invesitigation, it sparked 2 days of racial violence towards the black ncitizens of Tulsa. This event led to over 1,000 injuries and deaths, and the complete destruction of Black Wall Street, displacing over 9,000 people.
